Performance in detail

Every panel has a purpose.

The ALPFLUX Shirt uses mapped compression and ventilation so you feel locked in where it matters and free where you need range of motion.

Mapped compression knit

Mild compression wraps the upper back and chest to support posture during heavy lifts and backpack carry without a restrictive feel.

Trail vent underarm zones

Micro mesh channels under arms and along the sides move heat away fast on climbs while staying invisible from the outside.

Matte nature ready surface

A soft touch knit with a low sheen finish keeps the shirt looking like a normal tee in natural light and indoor settings.

Pack friendly shoulder seams

Offset seams prevent rubbing under backpack straps or barbell contact, keeping sessions and hikes irritation free.

Curved trail hem

Slight drop tail and side curve keep coverage when you hinge, sprint, or scramble over rocks.

Anti odor treatment

A permanent finish helps control odor so you can wear the shirt from gym to trail without needing a mid day change.
